Lion

It's huge and is also known as the King of the Jungle here in Africa.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Avarage Length: 1.4 - 2.5 meters
  • Avarage Lifespan: 10 - 14 years
  • Habitat: scrub, grasslands or open woodlands

A group of lions is called a pride. In a pride of lions, there are related females, their young, and one or two adult males. Groups of female lions often hunt together.
